Contact

How to reach me, and, just as honestly, what I can and can't help you with.

Home › Contact

First, the most important thing, said plainly so no one is disappointed later: I do not run a tour company. This site is one Javanese guide reading the listings and telling you honestly which tours fit. I do not own vans, I do not sell packages, and I am not a booking desk. That shapes what I can actually do for you here, so let me lay it out clearly.

What I can't help with

I can't book a tour for you. I have no inventory and no way to take a reservation. Every tour I recommend is run by an established operator, and you book it directly.

I can't plan your individual trip by email. I would love to, honestly, but guiding takes most of my days, and if I tried to answer everyone's itinerary questions one by one I would either stop guiding or stop sleeping. Instead, I have poured everything I would tell a friend into the Plan Your Trip page and the category hubs. That is the real version of my advice, written carefully, available to you any hour of the day.

To book a tour

We earn a commission when you book through our links on Viator (PID P00303273), at no extra cost to you.

Use the Viator links throughout this site. On each page I point you to the specific listings that pass my three checks, guide quality, safety record, and value, and the links take you straight to the operator's booking page on Viator, where your payment and reservation are handled securely. That is the whole mechanism: I tell you which tour fits, the link takes you there, you book directly. If you are not sure where to start, the Borobudur sunrise, Merapi and Prambanan full-day tour is where most first-timers begin, or browse the Borobudur and Plan Your Trip pages for the deeper guides.

For site feedback or corrections

This is where I genuinely want to hear from you. If a price has changed, a ticket rule has shifted, an operator has slipped, a link is broken, or I have simply got something wrong, tell me. Accuracy is the entire point of this site, and travellers on the ground often spot a change before I do. Corrections like that make the next reader's trip better, and I am grateful for every one.

You can reach me at rama@yogyakarta-temple-tours.com.

One honest note on what happens next: I read every message, but I can't reply to all of them, guiding takes most of my days. If you have flagged a correction, assume it has been seen and will be checked, even if a reply doesn't land in your inbox. It is not rudeness; it is just the arithmetic of one person on the slope of a volcano with a full guiding calendar. Thank you for understanding, and thank you for caring enough to write.
